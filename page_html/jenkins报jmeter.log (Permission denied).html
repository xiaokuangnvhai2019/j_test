jenkins报jmeter.log (Permission denied)

 

问题：jenkins执行jmeter的时候报无log权限

 

2019-08-30 21:00:17,306 main ERROR FileManager (jmeter.log) java.io.FileNotFoundException: jmeter.log (Permission denied) java.io.FileNotFoundException: jmeter.log (Permission denied)

 

 

 

解决方案：给日志加权限即可

进入jmeter的bin目录下执行



chmod 777 *.log
